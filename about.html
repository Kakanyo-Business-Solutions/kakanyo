Our Timeline

We've had an exceptionally phenomanal business journey of growth

2020

Acquisition

We have enjoyed steady exponential growth that has led to the expansion of our business through acquisitions. To enhance our offering, we've acquired KoenaConcepts along with its product, the SpanLula mobile platform. Following this acquisition, we've rebranded as Kakanyo, the holding company with a group of companies namely; Kakanyo Business Solutions (KBS), KoenaConcepts (KC) & SpanLula all operating as individual brands and subsidiary companies of Kakanyo.

2010

Growth

The business grew and diversified into other complementary areas including Business Process Management (BPM), Programme and Project Management and end-to-end ICT software and infrastructure services and solutions.

2004

Startup

In 2004, Kakanyo began as a specialist Business Intelligence (BI) consultancy. It was established with the purpose of providing tailored business services and turnkey IT solutions to the South African private and public sectors.
